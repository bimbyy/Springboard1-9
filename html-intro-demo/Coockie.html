How to make a Cookie Document

Ingredients

This is how to make a cookie follow the below instructions.

  1. 6 eggs
  2. 2 Cups of flour
  3. 2 Tablespoons of vanilla extract
  4. 1 Cup of sugar

Instructions

  1. Add eggs, flour and vanilla extract to mix
  2. Combine with sugar and baking powder and stir
  3. Eat half the cookie batter because it is delicious
  4. Take the rest of the cookie batter and make cookies
  5. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
